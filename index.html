1. Introduction

Welcome to Robokacija AI Lab ("we," "our," "us"), an educational and research-focused platform designed to assist students in learning robotics through personalized interactions. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our platform, particularly in the context of education and research.

By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, academic information, and feedback during learning sessions.
  • Learning Data: Information about your learning progress, responses to exercises, preferences, and feedback provided during interactions with Robokacija AI Lab.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected data including your device information, IP address, browser type, time spent on the platform, and pages accessed.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance the user experience and gather statistical information.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect data for two primary purposes:

  • Educational Support: To provide personalized learning experiences, adjust the complexity of tasks, and track progress.
  • Research: To support ongoing research related to your PhD study on educational assistants. This may include the analysis of anonymized data for research purposes, such as understanding how students interact with AI-based educational tools.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Personalize learning activities and provide appropriate challenges based on your skill level.
  • Improve the Robokacija AI Lab’s performance and educational approach.
  • Analyze interactions for educational research to inform your PhD research, ensuring the development of effective educational assistants.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and only share your data in the following ways:

  • Research Purposes: Anonymized data may be used in research publications, presentations, or reports as part of your PhD study.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers who help us maintain and improve our platform. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and cannot use your data for any other purposes.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your personal information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or regulatory inquiry.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for the duration of your use of Robokacija AI Lab and as long as it is necessary for educational or research purposes. If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us at ivan.kunovic@robokacija.hr.

8. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your data, subject to legal and research-related obligations.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data for certain purposes, such as marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at ivan.kunovic@robokacija.hr.

9. Research Ethics and Consent

As part of your participation in the Robokacija AI Lab for educational purposes and research, you may be asked to provide consent for the use of your anonymized data in research. Your participation is voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time without any negative consequences.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
